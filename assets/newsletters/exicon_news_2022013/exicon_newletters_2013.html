Success story

How to increase your hardware sales through a relevant software strategy?

Challenge

Given that locally relevant content drives downloads, thereby adding value and device sales, a large European Appstore operator needed to:

- Identify value added content to drive downloads

- Define a set of applications to target based upon their demographics

- Have a local approach in 20 markets

Solution

Define

Having confirmed the target demographics for the company as a whole as well as any individual market specifics, Exicon set up teams to approach all 20 markets.

Using our network of qualified developers and a network of global researchers we were able to define the top 5 brands in each category, both local and international application.

A review was then undertaken to establish the mobile status of the brands.

Results

The client received a list of target brands, by market with a break down of which brands had applications on the client platform and the associated developers, which brands had applications on alternative platforms and which would need to be encouraged to build applications.

"Locally relevant content increased downloads by up to 30%"
