10 steps to creating an app your users will love

There are so many examples out there of great mobile features that it is easy to get distracted from what really matters - both to you and to your users.

Understanding what is going on in your industry and with your competitors as well as learning how parallel industries are succeeding is a great place to start.

Mapping the journey to your business and user is next, taking into consideration the type of engagement your users are looking for.

Once you have a handle on this you can choose the features that do the best job of delivering the kind experience that you want your users to have.

Then list your requirements and find the best developer for the type of app or API you are trying to build.
