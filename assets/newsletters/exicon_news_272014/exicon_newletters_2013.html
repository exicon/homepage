World Cup Fever

This year's World Cup has extended beyond television & social networks to engage audiences on new mobile apps

Events like the world cup make it more clear than ever that a mobile app is not a one-size-fits-all kind of product.  This year, more than ever, hundreds of different news outlets, media companies, and developers have published apps aimed squarely at this single event.
These new apps haven't had any trouble finding an audience; several have shot up to the top of the iTunes and various Android app stores.  FIFA's experience with app crashes should just how much its traffic expectations were exceeded.
Here at Exicon, we've always encouraged our clients to think in terms of the App LifeCycle.  This forces them to form clearer expectations for how their app will be used and to understand their analytics well enough to spot opportunities for their next app.  We hope you're not only enjoying the World Cup for the football (or soccer), but also learning the lessons it has for your business.
